Agra

Agra is a city on the banks of the Yamuna river in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh, about 210 kilometres south of the national capital New Delhi and 320 km west of the state capital Lucknow.

Uttar Pradeshi thali with naan, dal, raita, and shahi paneer. Malaiyo, a dessert made from milk cream, a specialty of Varanasi. The samosa is a popular snack from Uttar Pradesh. Paan (betel leaves) being served with silver foil at Sarnath near Varanasi..

Kathak

Uttar Pradesh is the place where Kathak, one of the six foremost classical dances of India, flourished. The word Katha, from which the name is derived, means story.
