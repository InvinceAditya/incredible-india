Vagamon

Vagamon is an Indian hill station and a revenue village primarily located in Peerumedu taluk of Idukki district, and also Meenachil taluk and Kanjirappally taluk of Kottayam district in the state of Kerala, India.

Idli

Idli or idly is a type of savoury rice cake, originating from the Indian subcontinent, popular as breakfast foods in Eastern and Southern India and in Sri Lanka. The cakes are made by steaming a batter consisting of fermented black lentils and rice.

Mohiniyattam

Mohiniyattam is an Indian classical dance form that developed and remained popular in the state of Kerala. Kathakali is another classical dance form of Kerala.
