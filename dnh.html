Hirwan Garden

Green area with slides, a waterfall & animals like rabbits & ducks, popular for picnicking.

Nakshatra Garden

Nakshatra Garden is an Astro-themed garden, which has a large variety of plants and trees linked to zodiac signs. The garden has been designed according to the Indian system of astrology that links plants with the motion of different planets. The garden has a dedicated play area for kids.
