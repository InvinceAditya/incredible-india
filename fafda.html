Fafda

Fafda, is a favorite fried snack made with chickpea flour (Besan) from Gujarat

Jalebi is a sweet made with maida that is extremely popular in India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Iran and in some parts of Africa. For the uninitiated, jalebi is a sweet prepared from a dough which is dropped into hot oil giving it a crunchy outside and sweet inside texture which gives an amazing taste.
