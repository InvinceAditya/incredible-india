Kadmat Island

Kadmat Island, also known as Cardamom Island, is a coral island belonging to the Amindivi subgroup of islands of the Lakshadweep archipelago in India.

Maritime museum

A maritime museum is a museum specializing in the display of objects relating to ships and travel on large bodies of water. A subcategory of maritime museums are naval museums, which focus on navies and the military use of the sea

Thinnakara Island

Thinnakara is a treat reserved for very few humans. The remote island, has a beautiful lagoon separating it from Bangaram and Pitti islands.
